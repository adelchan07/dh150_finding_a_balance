About the Project

'Finding a Balance': a digital project analyzing the root of divergence between the opposing trends of a increased reliance on technology versus the growing popularity for 'technology cleaneses' within contemporary society.

Taking a step back, it is quite jarring to consider how much we as a soceity have become completely reliant on technology. Even with things as simple as directions, individuals were walking around with paper maps less than two decades ago. While today, majority of people cannot even get to their neighborhood grocery store without the help of Google Maps.

Though technology has proven countless times that it can improve our quality of life immensely,Â many have begun to notice the harmful effects of social media and a growing obsession with an altered reality. Recently, there has been a growing trend of 'technology cleanses' where individuals take a step back from a technologically integrated lifestyle through choices ranging from limiting screen time to ditching smart phones or the internet all-together.

Though Miller's experience is one of the extreme, his learnings prompt the need for discussions regarding how to create a healthy balance between connection and disconnection from technology.
